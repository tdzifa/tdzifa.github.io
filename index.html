About Me

I was born in a small private hospital in Dzorwulu, Accra in 1990. I have lived in different parts of Accra my whole life. I however spent three years in Cape Coast for my high school education and four years in Kumasi for university education.

Who am I?

I am a fun-loving lady who looks very quiet on the outside. You would however see the talkative in me once I get more comfortable around you. I love telling stories, especially to my family whom I so love being around. I am also fairly sportive. I enjoy watching "big" football games that is when Ghana is on the field!
I had my primary and junior high school education at Christ the King International School. This was followed by my senior high school education at Wesley Girls' High School and my university education at KNUST where I majored in Computer Science. I am currently an EIT at MEST.
